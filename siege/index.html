Description

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Siege is a tactical first person shooter which bases it's gameplay off of real time destruction physics. Floors, ceilings, and walls can be breached in order to give one team a tactical advantage over the other. Players are able to choose and customize their characters from a variety of international counter terrorist units (CTUs). Each operator has a unique gadget that is used to determine their playstyle. These range from extra armor plates to soak up damage, to headgear that allows you to see and track footprints.
